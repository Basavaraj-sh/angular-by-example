Global Injector


Dependency injection is a key aspect of Angular and is extremely useful. However, there are times when you may not have access to dependency injection. If you can't get to injection, then how can you make use of angular services?

Using the ngModules to your advantage, you can assign a global injector during your apps instantiation, which will be available to any code you run anywhere within your application. This make getting to the injector a matter of simply importing this globalInjector.
